MINUTES OF THE REGULAR MEETING OF THE TECHNICAL STEERING COMMITTEE

11 November 2018

A regular meeting of the Technical Steering Committee (the “TSC”) of the OpenAPI Initiative (“OAI”) was held on 08 November 2018 at 9:00 a.m. PT via conference call.

| Present | Not Present |
| --- | --- |
| Marsh Gardiner | Jeremy Whitlock |

-   Additional attendees: Henri Andrews (JSON), **Taylor Barnett** (Stoplight), Chuck Heazel (???), **Pedro Molina** (ISA) **Jonathan Stoikovitch** (Mulesoft), Phil Sturgeon (WeWork), and Jeff ErnstFriedman (Linux Foundation) Note: *\- Bold signifies employee of OAI member company at the time of the meeting*

Darrel Miller called the meeting to order and continued the discussion of the consideration of GitHub Issue: [#1736](https://github.com/OAI/OpenAPI-Specification/pull/1736) and the 'Alternative Schema' feature proposal to be included in a future release of the OpenAPI Spec.

Having exhausted the alloted time, the meeting was adjourned to begin again next week.
