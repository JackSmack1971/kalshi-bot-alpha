MINUTES OF THE REGULAR MEETING OF THE TECHNICAL STEERING COMMITTEE

20 September 2018

A regular meeting of the Technical Steering Committee (the “TSC”) of the OpenAPI Initiative (“OAI”) was held on 20 September 2018 at 9:00 a.m. PT via conference call.

| Present | Not Present |
| --- | --- |
| Marsh Gardiner | Ron Ratovsky |
| Darrel Miller | Jeremy Whitlock |
| Mike Ralphson |  |
| Uri Sarid |  |

-   Additional attendees: Henry Andrews (JSON), **John Boswell (Google)**, **Ted Epstein** (Reprezen), **Antonio Garrote** (Mulesoft), Chuck Heazel (WiSC Enterprises), Matt Jones (American Express), **Mike Kistler** (IBM), **Isabelle Mauny** (42Crunch), **Emmanuel Paraskakis** (Oracle), Justin Radcliffe (GM) **Jonathan Stoikovitch** (Mulesoft) and Jeff ErnstFriedman (Linux Foundation) Note: *\- Bold signifies employee of OAI member company at the time of the meeting*

Having quorum, Darrel Miller called the meeting to order at 9:05 am. The body to continue to consider GitHub Issue: [#1442](https://github.com/OAI/OpenAPI-Specification/issues/1442) and the 'overlay' feature proposal to be included in a future release of the OpenAPI Spec.

At 10am the body tabled the issue for briefly as Darrel Miller needed to step away. Miller raised the issue that with many members attending various conferences, it was suggested to cancel the next meeting, and without objection, the motion was approved.

The body then moved to open the floor for new participants to introduce themselves and allowed the conversation to continue on #1442. The body moved to create an action item for the next meeting to address the specific usecases for the overlays feature. Darrel Miller outlined a number of options on #1442 [in a comment](https://github.com/OAI/OpenAPI-Specification/issues/1442#issuecomment-423221341)

The body moved to adjourn at 10:15pm and will take up this issue again at the next meeting.
