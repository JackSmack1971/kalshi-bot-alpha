# Design Quality Rules

- Designs must stay tied to target users, jobs-to-be-done, and the approved MVP scope.
- Information architecture, user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and handoff artifacts must preserve one traceable story from problem to interface.
- Usability findings must stay visible in design outputs. Do not hide friction to keep a phase green.
- Design-system work should normalize repeated patterns, not introduce speculative component sprawl.
- Design artifacts are incomplete until they expose open questions, known compromises, and handoff constraints.
