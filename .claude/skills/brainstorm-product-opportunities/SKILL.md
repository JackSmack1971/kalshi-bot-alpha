# Brainstorm Product Opportunities

## Purpose

Generate a bounded opportunity catalog from founder intent, constraints, and domain context. Use at the start of discovery when the workflow needs distinct product opportunities before problem selection.

## Entry Conditions

- Use only in discovery. Do not use to approve market truth, scope an MVP, or plan implementation.
- Required upstream artifacts, approvals, and workflow context for this node are available.

## Required Inputs

See [Idea-to-MVP Common Skill Sections](../_shared/idea-to-mvp-common-sections.md#required-inputs) — no skill-specific additions.

## Procedure

1. Convert the supplied intent into 3 to 7 distinct product opportunities.
2. State each opportunity as a bounded problem-area statement.
3. Label the main assumptions and obvious constraints for each.
4. Note why each opportunity is distinct from the others.

## Permitted Tools

- `Read`, `Grep`, `Glob`

## Required Evidence

See [Idea-to-MVP Common Skill Sections](../_shared/idea-to-mvp-common-sections.md#required-evidence) — no skill-specific additions.

## Output Artifact Contract

Return:

- `opportunity_catalog`
- `assumptions`
- `constraints`
- `obvious_unknowns`

- Do not claim external evidence you do not have.
- Do not collapse multiple ideas into one vague opportunity.
- Do not recommend implementation details.
