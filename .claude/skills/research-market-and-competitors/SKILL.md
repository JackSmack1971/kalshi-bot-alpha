# Research Market And Competitors

## Purpose

Produce a bounded market and competitor report for an approved problem area, including alternatives, gaps, and research limitations. Use during discovery after problem validation and before value-proposition or core-problem selection.

## Entry Conditions

- Use only in discovery. Do not use to approve strategy, define MVP scope, or infer product-market fit from weak evidence.
- Required upstream artifacts, approvals, and workflow context for this node are available.

## Required Inputs

See [Idea-to-MVP Common Skill Sections](../_shared/idea-to-mvp-common-sections.md#required-inputs) — no skill-specific additions.

## Procedure

1. Define the relevant market and the practical alternatives users have today.
2. Identify direct competitors, substitutes, and notable gaps.
3. Separate verified facts, estimates, inferences, and unknowns.
4. Record source limitations and what still needs validation.

## Permitted Tools

- `Read`, `Grep`, `Glob`

Return:

- `market_competitor_report`
- `alternatives`
- `gaps`
- `research_limitations`

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Do not present inference as verified fact.
- Do not hide thin or low-credibility sourcing.
- Do not recommend implementation details.
